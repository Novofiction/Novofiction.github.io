Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with LexiApp? +

Simply download the app from the App Store or Google Play, create an account, and you're ready to start learning! The app will guide you through an initial assessment to customize your learning experience.

Can I use LexiApp offline? +

Yes, with a Premium subscription you can download lessons for offline use. This is perfect for learning on-the-go without using your data plan.

How does the family plan work? +

The family plan allows up to 5 family members to have their own personalized accounts under one subscription. Each member gets their own learning profile, progress tracking, and customized content.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime? +

Absolutely! You can cancel your subscription at any time. If you cancel during a free trial period, you won't be charged. If you cancel during a paid subscription period, you'll continue to have access until the end of your billing cycle.
