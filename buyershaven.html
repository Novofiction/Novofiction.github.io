What is The Buyer's Haven?

The Buyer’s Haven is a short-term home for people who’ve sold their house but can’t move into their new one yet. It gives you a furnished place to live, weekly flexibility, and time to wait without pressure, so you can pause, breathe, and live normally until your next move is ready.

A calm, fully furnished home designed specifically for buyers waiting to move.

This two-bedroom Buyer’s Haven is located in Watton, Norfolk and offers everything needed to live comfortably while your new home is being finalised — without monthly contracts, pressure, or nightly Airbnb costs.
