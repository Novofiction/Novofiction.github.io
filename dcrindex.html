About

DCR Music is the latest addition to Attleborough town centre, under the ownership of local band member Steve Whetton. We have a passion for music, and we know you have too. If there is anything you would like us to supply or have any suggestions on how we can improve our service please contact us. Our shop offers a wide range of musical equipment and accessories, from guitars, amplifiers and drum kits to plectrums, guitar strings, drum sticks and music books, for both learners and experienced musicians alike. We are an official LAG guitar dealership as well as suppliers of Carlsboro amplification/PA systems, Shure, Epiphone, Vintage, Stagg and other well known manufacturers. There is a wide range of student Brass/Woodwind instruments and discounts on selected items. For further information contact Luke on 01953 458039 or email us at info@dcrmusic.co.uk

Guitar Lessons

DCR music are now offering professional guitar tuition for all levels. Choose either from in-house lessons or learn in the comfort of your own home. Phone or email us for further details and pricing.
