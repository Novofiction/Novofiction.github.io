soundperfect ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our apps and related services ("Services").

1. Information We Collect

We collect email addresses during user registration. We do not collect or store payment information. A unique user ID (UUID) may be collected to match your progess to your account. Audio recordings (temporarily, during analysis only). Technical device information (like device integrity status) for security and fraud prevention purposes may be collected.

  • To send marketing communications only if you have opted in

3. Audio Recording and Processing

When you use our services, we process your audio recordings to provide feedback:

  • Temporary Storage: Recordings are briefly stored on your device during processing, then automatically deleted. On Android, a single recording may persist in protected app storage (not accessible to any other app) until uninstall.
  • Third-Party Analysis: Audio may be sent to third-parties for scoring. Audio thus sent is processed in real-time in memory only and deleted immediately after analysis - no audio is stored on servers.
  • What We Keep: Only your numerical scores and assessment results are saved to track your progress. We never store the actual audio recordings.

Audio processing may occur on servers that may be located outside your country. By using our pronunciation features, you consent to this temporary processing.
